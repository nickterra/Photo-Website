Business Opportunities

Portraits USA, LLC is a growing business, and is currently seeking those with the determination and drive to successfully run their own business. We have business ownership opportunities available in many areas of the country.Our system has a proven track record. While like any business venture there is no guarantee of success, because of our unique licensing program and systematic approach to business, our Independent Licensees have an exceptional success rate running their own business as an independent agent of Portraits USA, LLC.

We offer the following to our Independent Licensees:

  1. Low initial investment compared to most Franchise/Independent business ventures.
  2. No formal business location required, we use mobile photography studios, in pre-existing locations with retail accounts we already have in place, not requiring long term leases.
  3. Existing locations to sell portraits across the country, in many of the nation's largest retailers.
  4. Initial training and development assistance.
  5. Work with one of the leading photo-labs.
  6. Ongoing support and meetings with both Portraits USA, LLC, and existing Independent Licensees for development, discussion, recognition and guidance.
  7. Our proven system covering sales, photography, and management.
  8. Much, much more.... best of all, you start earning money almost immediately!
